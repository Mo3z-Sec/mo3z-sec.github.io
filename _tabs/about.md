## 🛠️ My Setup

I run a **homelab** built from scratch for learning and practicing offensive security:

- **Proxmox VE** hypervisor on a Lenovo desktop — hosting all my VMs and containers

- **MacBook** as my main management workstation
- **Isolated lab network** for safe exploitation practice
- Self-hosted services including **Pi-hole**, **Nextcloud**, and **DuckDNS**
